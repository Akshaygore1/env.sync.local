env-sync vs dotenvx

Launch-grade local secrets sync with explicit trust modes and zero central control plane.

dotenvx positions itself as a secure dotenv from the creator of dotenv, emphasizing cross-platform runtime usage, multi-environment support, and encrypted env workflows. env-sync solves a different problem: syncing secrets across local peers with discovery and mode-specific transport.

Dimensionenv-syncdotenvx
Primary focusDistributed sync across machines on local networkRun app commands with env injection and encrypted env management
Peer networkingBuilt-in mDNS discovery + SSH/mTLS transportNo peer discovery or network synchronization layer
Trust/security modelThree explicit operation modesEncrypted env files + key-driven runtime workflows
Best fitKeep multiple hosts in sync without central serverStandardize local/CI env loading across languages
