env-sync vs Doppler

Doppler provides centralized cloud secrets workflows via its CLI and hosted platform. env-sync intentionally focuses on decentralized local-peer sync without a central control plane.

Dimensionenv-syncDoppler
Control planeNone (distributed peers)Centralized SaaS/organization config model
Peer syncAutomatic mDNS discovery + syncNot LAN peer sync focused
Operational modelLocal network, offline-friendlyCloud-oriented secret distribution workflows
Best fitHomelab/dev LAN fleet consistencyCentralized team governance and cloud integrations
